Health & Safety


iPhone 17 Pro and iPhone 17 Pro Max are designed with your safety in mind. All materials used meet strict international safety standards and are free from harmful substances. The device includes built-in features to help you manage screen time and reduce blue light exposure with Night Shift mode. Always use your iPhone in well-ventilated areas and avoid exposing it to extreme temperatures. For your safety, use only certified charging accessories and keep the device away from water unless specifically rated for water resistance. If you experience discomfort while using your iPhone, take regular breaks and consult with a healthcare professional.
